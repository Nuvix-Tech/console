# Providers

Nuvix allows you to connect to a variety of third-party messaging providers to deliver push notifications, emails, and SMS messages to your users. Before you can deliver messages, you must connect to a messaging provider.

## Push notifications

Send push notifications, which are little notification messages that appear on a user's browser or device to alert them of events or updates. Configure one of the following providers to send push notifications.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
<Card title="APNS" icon="smartphone" href="/products/messaging/apns">
Send push notifications to apps on Apple devices through Apple Push Notification service (APNs).
</Card>

<Card title="FCM" icon="message-square" href="/products/messaging/fcm">
Send push notifications to Android, Apple, or Web app with Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM).
</Card>
</CardGroup>
